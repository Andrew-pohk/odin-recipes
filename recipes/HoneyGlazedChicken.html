Honey Glazed Chicken

Easy to make Honey Glazed Chicken! prep time takes 10
minutes, cook time takes 10 minutes, the whole meal takes
a total of 20 minutes. This meal is very health and easy to
make, the serving size is 4 for the ingredients below.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces
