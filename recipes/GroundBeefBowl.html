Easy Korena Ground Beef Bowl

This is easy to make Korean Ground Beef Bowl. This bowl
has ingredients that could be easily changed for your liking.
Not only that but it healthy and delicious. The prep time
takes 10 minutes. the cooking time takes 15 minutes, so the
total time takes 25 minutes for 4 serving sized food.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 5 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 tablespoons freshly freshly grated ginger
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 6 green onions, chopped divided
  • 4 cups hot cooked brown rice
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds
